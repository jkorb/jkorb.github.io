About

I’m a mathematical philosopher who mainly works at the intersection of logic, metaphysics, and epistemology. I also ❤ the (philosophy of) mathematics.

Research

Research interests: hyperintensionality • mathematical structuralism • structural realism • deontic logic • metaphysical grounding

Editorial work: I recently joined the Journal of Philosophical Logic as associate editor.
