Johannes Korbmacher

I’m a mathematical philosopher who mainly works at the intersection of logic, metaphysics, and epistemology. I also ❤ the (philosophy of) mathematics.

I’m currently an Assistant Professor in Theoretical Philosophy (tenured) at Utrecht University. Before that, I did my PhD (in philosophy) at the Munich Center for Mathematical Philosophy (MCMP).
