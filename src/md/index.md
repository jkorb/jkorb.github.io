## Johannes Korbmacher

I'm a [mathematical philosopher](https://doi.org/10.1111/meta.12029) who mainly works at the intersection of logic, metaphysics, and epistemology. I also &#10084; the (philosophy of) mathematics.

I'm currently an Assistant Professor in Theoretical Philosophy (tenured) at [Utrecht University](https://www.uu.nl/en/organisation/department-of-philosophy-and-religious-studies). Before that, I did my PhD (in philosophy) at the [Munich Center for Mathematical Philosophy (MCMP)](https://www.mcmp.philosophie.uni-muenchen.de/index.html).

You can read more details in my academic CV ([PDF](https://github.com/jkorb/cv/releases/latest/download/cv.pdf) • [LaTeX](https://github.com/jkorb/cv)).
